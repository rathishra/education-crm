Modules

12 Powerful Modules. One Unified Platform.

Every department in your institution — connected, automated, and transparent.

01
Admission & Enquiry CRM

Capture leads from website, walk-ins, calls, and campaigns. Assign counsellors, track follow-up stages, and auto-send reminders — all from one view.

02
Student Information System

Centralized student profiles with academic history, documents, attendance, behavioural notes, and parent contact — all accessible by authorized roles.

03
Fee & Payment Management

Configure complex fee structures, apply scholarships, collect online via Razorpay/PayU, generate receipts, and track defaulters with automated alerts.

04
Attendance Management

Biometric, RFID, and manual attendance with real-time dashboards. Auto SMS to parents on absenteeism, and minimum attendance enforcement.

05
Academic & Timetable

Subject-faculty assignment, semester/year planning, automatic clash detection, and digital timetable publishing to students and parents.

06
Exam & Result Management

Schedule exams, generate hall tickets, configure mark sheets, manage grace marks, compute results, and publish online or print.

07
Hostel Management

Room allocation, amenity tracking, mess management, warden portal, visitor log, and hostel fee integration with the main fee module.

08
Transport Management

Route planning, vehicle assignment, driver records, GPS tracking integration, and transport fee collection with parent notifications.

09
Library Management

Book catalogue, RFID-based issue/return, due date alerts, fine collection, and seamless integration with the student profile.

10
Placement & Career Cell

Company registrations, campus drive scheduling, student application tracking, offer letter management, and placement analytics.

11
HR & Payroll

Faculty and staff records, attendance, leave management, salary structure configuration, payslips, and PF/ESI compliance.

12
Analytics & Reports

Executive dashboards, custom report builder, 50+ pre-built reports, trend charts, and scheduled email delivery to stakeholders.
