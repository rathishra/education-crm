Our Story

We Are Building the Future of Institutions

Founded with a singular mission — to digitise and empower the education and healthcare sectors of India with world-class enterprise software.

Our Journey

From a Startup Dream to 500+ Institutions

Your Company Name was founded in 2018 by a team of technologists and domain experts who witnessed firsthand the operational chaos in Indian colleges and clinics — mountains of paperwork, missed follow-ups, fee leakage, and zero analytics.

We set out to build enterprise-grade software that was affordable for Indian institutions — not a sprawling ERP that takes 2 years to implement, but a lean, powerful, intuitive platform that gets you live in days.

Today, over 500 educational institutions and 150 clinics trust our platform to manage millions of records daily. And we're just getting started.
